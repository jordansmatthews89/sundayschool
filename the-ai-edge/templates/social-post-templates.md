# Social post templates — LinkedIn & X (Twitter)

Use these each week when you publish an issue. Paste into Claude with the bracketed info filled in, or use as-is and swap the placeholders.

---

## Template 1: LinkedIn (issue teaser)

**Paste into Claude:** "Write a short LinkedIn post (3–4 sentences, no hashtag pile) that teases this week’s newsletter without giving everything away. Tone: professional but warm. Include a soft CTA to subscribe. Newsletter focus this week: [Tool of the week name] and [one quick hit or prompt theme]."

**Or use this and fill in:**
- This week in The AI Edge: [Tool of the week] — why I use it and how you can too. Plus [X] more tools and a copy-paste prompt for [use case]. Link in comments if you want the full issue every week.

---

## Template 2: X / Twitter (thread starter or single tweet)

**Single tweet:** "Issue just went out: [Tool of the week] + 4 quick hits + one prompt you can use today. Subscribe for the full thing every week: [link]"

**Or paste into Claude:** "Write 1–2 short tweets (under 280 chars each) to promote this week’s newsletter. Focus: [tool of the week]. Include a subscribe link. No hashtags."

---

## Template 3: Prompt-of-the-week standalone

**Use when the prompt is especially strong:** "Write a LinkedIn post that shares this week’s prompt as a free tip. Give the prompt name and a one-sentence benefit, then paste the prompt in a code block or quote. End with: ‘Get a new prompt like this every week — link in comments.’"

---

## Template 4: Quick CTA (any platform)

**Fill in and post:** "One email a week. The best AI tools and prompts. No fluff. [Link to signup]"

---

## Where to post

- **LinkedIn:** 1x per issue (day of send or next morning). Optional: 1–2 more posts per week with a single tip or prompt.
- **X:** 1x per issue + quote-tweet or reply with a key line from the issue.
- **Other:** Add your signup link to bio and pin a “Subscribe” post.

---

## Claude meta-prompt (all-in-one)

**Paste this into Claude each week with your issue focus:**

"You're promoting the weekly newsletter 'The AI Edge' (AI tools and prompts for professionals). This week’s issue features: [tool of the week], [quick hits theme], and [prompt of the week topic].

Generate:
1. One LinkedIn post (3–4 sentences, professional, soft CTA to subscribe).
2. Two Twitter/X options (each under 280 characters) to announce the issue.
3. One 'prompt of the week' standalone post for LinkedIn that gives the prompt name and benefit and invites people to subscribe for more.

Include [INSERT LINK] where the signup link should go. No hashtag stacks."

Replace [INSERT LINK] with your real signup URL before posting.
