# Templates — Family Faith

Reusable prompts for running the newsletter with AI.

| File | Use when |
|------|----------|
| **weekly-newsletter-prompt.md** | Every week when drafting the newsletter. Paste into Claude with that week’s passage, main point, questions, verse, and family take-home; paste the output into beehiiv. |
| **social-post-templates.md** | Every week after you send. Use the LinkedIn/X templates or the meta-prompt to get posts from Claude, then publish. |

## Weekly workflow

1. **Prep day (e.g. Monday):** Pick a lesson from `../curriculum/lessons/` or a passage + idea. Use `weekly-newsletter-prompt.md` — feed Claude the inputs; get the draft; schedule in beehiiv.
2. **Send day:** Issue goes out.
3. **Same day or next:** Use `social-post-templates.md` — get 1 LinkedIn post + 1–2 tweets from Claude; post with your signup link.

You can also ask Claude to suggest a passage and discussion questions for the week, then plug that into the weekly prompt.
