# Series: Fruit of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22–23)

9 weeks — one fruit per week. Same passage each time; focus shifts to that week’s fruit.

**Anchor verse:** “But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control; against such things there is no law.” — Galatians 5:22–23 (ESV)

---

## Weekly breakdown

| Week | Fruit | Key idea | Optional passage |
|------|--------|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | Love | God’s love for us; we love others | 1 John 4:7–12; John 13:34–35 |
| 2 | Joy | Joy in God, not circumstances | John 15:9–11; James 1:2–4 |
| 3 | Peace | Peace with God and with others | Philippians 4:4–7; John 14:27 |
| 4 | Patience | Waiting on God; patience with people | Psalm 40:1; James 5:7–8 |
| 5 | Kindness | Goodness in action toward others | Ephesians 4:32; Luke 6:35 |
| 6 | Goodness | Doing what’s right; integrity | Matthew 5:16; 2 Thessalonians 1:11 |
| 7 | Faithfulness | Trustworthy; loyal to God and others | Proverbs 3:3; 2 Timothy 2:13 |
| 8 | Gentleness | Strength under control; how we treat people | Matthew 11:29; Galatians 6:1 |
| 9 | Self-control | Spirit-led choices; saying no to sin | 1 Corinthians 9:24–27; Titus 2:11–12 |

Use the lesson template for each week; the sample lesson in `lessons/` is Week 1 (Love).
