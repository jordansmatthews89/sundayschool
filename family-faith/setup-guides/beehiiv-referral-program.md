# beehiiv Referral Program — Family Faith

Subscribers share a unique link; when someone signs up through it, they get a reward and you get growth.

---

## 1. Turn on the referral program

1. In beehiiv, go to **Growth → Referrals**.
2. Click **Enable referral program**.
3. Choose your main audience/list.

---

## 2. Set the reward

**What subscribers get when they refer someone:**

- **Free:** "Refer 3 families, get a thank-you in the next issue" or "Refer 5, get our free Family Discussion Prompts PDF."
- **Paid product:** "Refer 5 subscribers, get the Family Devotionals Pack free" — send a Gumroad discount code or gift link.
- **Premium:** If you add a paid tier later, "Refer 3, get one month free."

**Configure:**

- Number of referrals required (e.g. 3 or 5).
- Reward description (one sentence on the referral page).
- How you’ll deliver the reward (manual email with code/link, or automated if you set up a code).

---

## 3. Customize the referral page

- **Headline** — e.g. "Share Family Faith and get [reward]."
- **Description** — "Your friends get weekly family lessons and devotionals. You get [reward] after [X] signups."
- **Branding** — Match your newsletter colors and logo.

---

## 4. Add the referral block to your newsletter

1. Go to **Settings → Email template** (or **Design**).
2. In the footer, add the **Referral** block if available.
3. Or add a line: "Know other families who’d love this? Share your link: [Referral link] — refer 5 and get [reward]."

---

## 5. Track and reward

- In **Growth → Referrals** you’ll see who referred how many.
- When someone hits the required number, send their reward (product link, discount code, or shoutout).

---
