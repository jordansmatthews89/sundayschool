# Family devotional template

*Short (10–15 min) family time: read, talk, pray together. Use once a week or a few times a week.*

---

## [Title]

**Passage:**  
**One sentence for kids:** (What we’re learning in one line.)

---

## Read (2 min)

[Reference]. Have a parent or older kid read. Younger kids can repeat the main verse after you.

---

## Talk (5 min)

*Everyone answers. Keep it simple.*

1. What did we just read? (Let kids say it in their own words.)
2. What does that mean for us? (One sentence.)
3. **This week:** What’s one way we can live this as a family?

---

## Pray (1–2 min)

*One sentence each, or one person prays for the family.*

- **Prompt:** [e.g. “Thank God for [truth from the passage]; ask Him to help us [application].”]

---

*Optional: sing a short worship song or say the memory verse together.*
