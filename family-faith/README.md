# Family Faith — Newsletter + Digital Product Business

**Model:** Same as The AI Edge — weekly newsletter on beehiiv, digital products on Gumroad, referral program, AI to write most content. **Subject:** Christian curriculum and family Bible studies only.

**Audience:** Parents, grandparents, and church volunteers who want ready-to-use family lessons, devotionals, and discussion prompts without the prep.

**Revenue:** Newsletter (beehiiv Ad Network when you hit scale), digital products (curriculum packs, devotional packs), **physical store** (your designs, third-party fulfillment — no inventory), optional affiliate (Bibles, books).

---

## Project structure

| Folder | What it’s for |
|--------|----------------|
| **setup-guides/** | beehiiv, Gumroad, referral, **store + fulfillment** — step-by-step for this brand |
| **content/** | Welcome sequence, Issue #1, Gumroad product copy (curriculum/devotionals) |
| **landing-page/** | Newsletter signup page (family faith value prop) |
| **templates/** | Weekly newsletter prompt + social post templates (faith topic) |

---

## Quick start

1. **beehiiv** — Use `setup-guides/beehiiv-setup.md`. Publication name: **Family Faith** (or The Sunday Table). Tagline: ready-to-use family Bible lessons and devotionals, every week.
2. **Welcome sequence** — Add the 3 emails from `content/welcome-sequence/` to a Subscriber added automation.
3. **Gumroad** — Use `setup-guides/gumroad-setup.md` and `content/gumroad-products.md` to list your curriculum/devotional products.
4. **Landing page** — Host `landing-page/index.html` or use beehiiv’s hosted signup page; embed form.
5. **Issue #1** — Paste `content/issues/issue-001-draft.md` into beehiiv and schedule.
6. **Weekly** — Use `templates/weekly-newsletter-prompt.md`; feed it a passage + lesson idea and get the draft.

Content lives in `../curriculum/` (lessons, scope-and-sequence, devotionals). This folder is the **business** (newsletter, products, landing, automation).
