# Gumroad Setup Guide — The AI Edge

Use this guide to create your Gumroad account and list your first three digital products.

---

## 1. Create your Gumroad account

1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com) and click **Start selling**.
2. Sign up with Google or email.
3. Complete profile: add a display name, profile image (optional), and short bio (e.g. "Creator of The AI Edge — weekly AI tools and prompts.").
4. Connect a payout method (Stripe or PayPal) under **Settings → Payouts**.

---

## 2. Create your products

Create three products. For each one:

1. Click **New product**.
2. Choose **Digital product**.
3. Use the **title**, **description**, and **price** from `content/gumroad-products.md` in this repo (copy-paste and tweak if needed).
4. Upload the product file(s):
   - **100 Power Prompts Pack** — Upload a PDF or Notion/Google Doc link. You can generate the prompts with Claude and export as PDF.
   - **AI Productivity OS** — Upload a Notion template link (duplicate template, share link) or export as PDF + attach.
   - **Your First Week With AI** — Upload a PDF of the mini-guide (write in Google Docs or Notion, export PDF).
5. Set **Price** and **Currency** (USD recommended).
6. Under **Advanced**, enable **Allow customers to pay what they want** only if you want a pay-what-you-want option; for fixed pricing, leave it off.
7. Click **Publish** and copy the product URL.

---

## 3. Link Gumroad to your newsletter

- Add your Gumroad store URL to your beehiiv newsletter footer (Settings → Newsletter → Footer).
- In Welcome Email 3 (`content/welcome-sequence/email-3-products.md`), replace each `[Link to Gumroad]` with the exact product URL for that product.
- Optionally create a **Gumroad overlay** or **store page** and link to it from your landing page ("See all products").

---

## 4. Optional: Custom domain

In Gumroad **Settings → Custom domain**, you can use a subdomain like `shop.theaiedge.co` for your store. Not required to launch.
