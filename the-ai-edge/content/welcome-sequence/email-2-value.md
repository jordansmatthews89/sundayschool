**Prompt: "Weekly planning in 5 minutes"**

I'm [your role, e.g. a marketing lead]. Every [Monday morning], I need to plan my week in 5 minutes.  

Do this:  
1. List my top 3 priorities for the week (based on [your main goal]).  
2. For each priority, give me one concrete action I can do in the next 24 hours.  
3. Add one thing I should say no to or defer.  
4. Keep the whole response under 200 words.  

Output in a simple numbered list. No intro, no fluff.
