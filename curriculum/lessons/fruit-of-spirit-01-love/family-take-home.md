# Family take-home — Love (Fruit of the Spirit, Week 1)

*Print or email this so families can continue the lesson at home (dinner, bedtime, or family worship).*

---

## This week’s verse

**“Beloved, let us love one another, for love is from God.”** — 1 John 4:7 (ESV)

Say it together once at the start of your family time.

---

## Read together (2–3 min)

**1 John 4:7–11.** Have one person read, or take turns by verse.

---

## Talk about it (5–7 min)

Everyone can answer. No wrong answers.

1. Where does love come from? (From God.)
2. How did God show His love to us? (He sent Jesus.)
3. Who is someone in our family we can love better this week? What’s one way we can show it?
4. **As a family:** What’s one thing we’ll do together to show love (e.g. one chore we all help with, one kind word we each say every day)?

---

## Do it together (once this week)

- **Love in action:** Each person picks one way they’ll show love to someone in your family (e.g. help with a chore, say “I love you” at bedtime, share something). At the end of the week, ask: Did we do it? How did it go?

---

## Pray (1 min)

Thank God for loving us first. Ask Him to help us love each other at home and others outside our family this week.
