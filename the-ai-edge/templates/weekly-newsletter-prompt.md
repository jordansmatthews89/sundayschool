# Weekly newsletter — Claude prompt template

Use this every week (e.g. Monday). Paste into Claude and fill the bracketed parts. Then paste the output into beehiiv and schedule.

---

**Copy from here (including the prompt) — paste into Claude:**

---

You write a weekly newsletter called "The AI Edge." It goes to busy professionals who want the best AI tools, workflows, and prompts — no fluff.

**This week’s inputs:**
- Tool of the week (deep dive): [paste the name and 2–3 sentences about why it’s useful and how to use it]
- Quick hits (3–4 tools, one sentence each): [paste tool names and one-line descriptions]
- Prompt of the week: [paste the prompt title and the full copy-paste prompt with placeholders]

**Format requirements:**
1. Start with a 1–2 sentence intro that tees up the tool of the week.
2. **Tool of the Week** — One subhead, then 2 short paragraphs: why it matters, how to use it. Include the link.
3. **Quick Hits** — Bullet or short list, one line per tool with link.
4. **Prompt of the Week** — Subhead, then one short intro sentence, then the prompt in a code/quote block so readers can copy it.
5. End with a one-line sign-off and “[Your name] / The AI Edge.”
6. Total length: ~500–700 words. Tone: helpful, direct, no hype.

**Output:** Write the full newsletter body only (no subject line suggestions unless I ask). Use clear section headings. No placeholder text — use the exact tool names, links, and prompt I provided.

---

**After Claude responds:** Copy the body into beehiiv. Add your subject line (e.g. "The one AI tool I'm using every day" or "Issue #X: [Tool name] + 4 quick hits"). Schedule for your usual send day.
