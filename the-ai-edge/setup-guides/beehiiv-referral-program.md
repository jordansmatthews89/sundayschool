# beehiiv Referral Program — Setup Guide

The referral program lets subscribers share a unique link. When someone signs up through it, they get a reward and you get growth without paid ads.

---

## 1. Turn on the referral program

1. In beehiiv, go to **Growth → Referrals** (or **Audience → Referrals** depending on your plan).
2. Click **Enable referral program** or **Get started**.
3. Choose which **audience/list** the program applies to (usually your main newsletter list).

---

## 2. Set the reward

**What subscribers get when they refer someone:**

- **Free option:** "Refer 3 friends, get a thank-you shoutout in the next issue" or "Refer 5, get our [free resource]."
- **Paid product:** "Refer 5 subscribers, get the 100 Power Prompts Pack free" — you send them a Gumroad discount code or gift link.
- **Premium access:** If you add a paid tier later, "Refer 3, get one month free."

**What you need to configure:**

- **Number of referrals required** (e.g. 3 or 5).
- **Reward description** (one short sentence shown on the referral page).
- **How you’ll deliver the reward** (manual: you email them; or automated if you use a discount code / link).

---

## 3. Customize the referral page

- **Headline** — e.g. "Share The AI Edge and get [reward]."
- **Short description** — Why they should share (e.g. "Your friends get the best AI tools every week. You get [reward] after [X] signups.").
- **Branding** — Match your newsletter colors and logo.

Subscribers will see their **unique referral link** in every email (if you add the referral block to your template) or on a dedicated referral page you link to.

---

## 4. Add the referral block to your newsletter

1. Go to **Settings → Email template** (or **Design**).
2. In the footer or after the main content, add the **Referral** block/widget if available.
3. This shows each reader their personal link and the reward. Save the template.

If your template doesn’t have a referral block, add a line in your footer: “Love this? Share your link: [Referral link] — refer 5 friends and get [reward].”

---

## 5. Track and reward referrals

- In **Growth → Referrals** you’ll see who referred how many people.
- When someone hits the required number, send their reward (product link, discount code, or shoutout) manually, or use automation (e.g. Zapier/Make) if you scale.

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Referral program enabled for your main list
- [ ] Reward chosen and number of referrals set (e.g. 5 → free prompt pack)
- [ ] Referral page copy and branding updated
- [ ] Referral block or link added to newsletter footer/template
- [ ] Process for delivering the reward (manual or code) decided and noted
