# Gumroad Setup Guide — Family Faith

Use this guide to create your Gumroad account and list your curriculum and devotional products.

---

## 1. Create your Gumroad account

1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com) and click **Start selling**.
2. Sign up with Google or email.
3. Complete profile: display name, profile image (optional), short bio (e.g. "Creator of Family Faith — weekly family Bible lessons and devotionals.").
4. Connect a payout method (Stripe or PayPal) under **Settings → Payouts**.

---

## 2. Create your products

Create the products listed in `content/gumroad-products.md`. For each:

1. Click **New product**.
2. Choose **Digital product**.
3. Use the **title**, **description**, and **price** from that file.
4. Upload the product file(s):
   - **Family Devotionals Pack** — PDF (or link to Notion/Google Doc) with 12–52 short family devotionals. Use content from `../curriculum/devotionals/` and expand with the template.
   - **Fruit of the Spirit Family Curriculum** — PDF bundle of the 9-week series (leader guides + family take-homes) from `../curriculum/lessons/` and `../curriculum/scope-and-sequence/fruit-of-the-spirit.md`.
   - **Starter Lesson Bundle** — PDF of 3–5 ready-to-use lessons (e.g. Love, Jesus Calms the Storm) with leader guide + student sheet + family take-home from `../curriculum/lessons/`.
5. Set **Price** and **Currency** (USD).
6. Click **Publish** and copy the product URL.

---

## 3. Link Gumroad to your newsletter

- Add your Gumroad store URL to your beehiiv newsletter footer.
- In Welcome Email 3 (`content/welcome-sequence/email-3-products.md`), replace each `[Link to Gumroad]` with the real product URL.
- In weekly issues, link to relevant products when you mention a lesson or series.

---

## 4. Optional: Custom domain

In Gumroad **Settings → Custom domain**, you can use a subdomain like `shop.familyfaith.co`. Not required to launch.
