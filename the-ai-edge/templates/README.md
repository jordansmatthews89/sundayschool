# Templates — The AI Edge

Reusable prompts and copy for running the newsletter with AI.

| File | Use when |
|------|----------|
| **weekly-newsletter-prompt.md** | Every week when drafting the newsletter. Paste into Claude with that week’s tools and prompt; paste the output into beehiiv. |
| **social-post-templates.md** | Every week after you send. Use the LinkedIn/X templates or the meta-prompt to get posts from Claude, then publish. |

## Weekly workflow (recap)

1. **Monday (or your prep day):** Use `weekly-newsletter-prompt.md` — feed Claude the tool of the week, quick hits, and prompt; get the draft; schedule in beehiiv.
2. **Send day:** Issue goes out.
3. **Same day or next:** Use `social-post-templates.md` — get 1 LinkedIn post + 1–2 tweets from Claude; post with your signup link.

You can also ask Claude: "Find me 5 trending AI tools from the past 7 days with descriptions and use cases" to get the raw material for step 1.
