# Social post templates — Family Faith (LinkedIn & X)

Use these when you publish an issue. Paste into Claude with the bracketed info filled in, or use as-is.

---

## Template 1: LinkedIn (issue teaser)

**Paste into Claude:** "Write a short LinkedIn post (3–4 sentences) that teases this week’s Family Faith newsletter without giving the whole lesson. Tone: warm, for parents and families. Include a soft CTA to subscribe. This week’s focus: [lesson topic or verse]."

**Or use this and fill in:**
- This week in Family Faith: [one sentence about the lesson]. Plus a verse to say together and one family take-home. Link in comments if you want the full thing every week.

---

## Template 2: X / Twitter

**Single tweet:** "This week’s family faith lesson: [topic]. One verse, one question, one at-home idea. Subscribe for the full thing every week: [link]"

**Or paste into Claude:** "Write 1–2 short tweets (under 280 chars each) to promote this week’s Family Faith newsletter. Focus: [lesson topic]. Include subscribe link. No hashtags."

---

## Template 3: Verse-of-the-week standalone

**Use when the verse is especially shareable:** "Write a LinkedIn post that shares this week’s verse and one question families can ask at the table. End with: ‘Get a new lesson like this every week — link in comments.’"

---

## Template 4: Simple CTA

**Fill in and post:** "One email a week. Ready-to-use family Bible lessons. No prep. [Link to signup]"

---

## Where to post

- **LinkedIn:** 1x per issue (day of send or next morning). Good for parents and church volunteers.
- **X:** 1x per issue; optional quote-tweet with the verse or main point.
- **Facebook:** If your audience is there, same as LinkedIn — one post per issue with link.

---

## Claude meta-prompt (all-in-one)

**Paste into Claude each week:**

"You're promoting the weekly newsletter 'Family Faith' (ready-to-use family Bible lessons and devotionals). This week’s issue features: [lesson topic], verse [reference], and [one-line take-home].

Generate:
1. One LinkedIn post (3–4 sentences, warm, for parents, soft CTA to subscribe).
2. Two Twitter/X options (each under 280 characters) to announce the issue.
3. One 'verse of the week' standalone post for LinkedIn — share the verse and one question, invite people to subscribe for more.

Include [INSERT LINK] where the signup link should go. No hashtag stacks."

Replace [INSERT LINK] with your real signup URL before posting.
