# Family devotional — Love one another

*About 10 minutes. Good for dinner or before bed.*

---

## Love one another

**Passage:** 1 John 4:7, 11  
**One sentence for kids:** God loves us first; we get to love each other.

---

## Read (2 min)

**1 John 4:7** — “Beloved, let us love one another, for love is from God.”  
**1 John 4:11** — “Beloved, if God so loved us, we also ought to love one another.”

Have a parent read. Then say the first verse together twice.

---

## Talk (5 min)

1. Where does love come from? (From God.)
2. What should we do because God loved us? (Love one another.)
3. **This week:** What’s one way we can show love to someone in our family? (Each person can name one thing.)

---

## Pray (1–2 min)

Thank God for loving us. Ask Him to help us love each other at home and at school/work this week.

---

*Optional: Say the verse one more time together.*
