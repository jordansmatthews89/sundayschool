**Hello Families!** This week, our focus is on love — the first fruit of the Spirit. We’ll explore how God’s love for us empowers us to love others in our lives. 

## Lesson of the week
**Passage:** Galatians 5:22–23; 1 John 4:7–12  
**Main point:** God is love, and He loves us first. When we know His love, we can love others.

**Discussion Questions:**  
- Why does John say “whoever loves has been born of God”? What does that mean for us?  
- How did God show His love in this passage?  
- Who is someone in your life that’s easy to love? Who’s harder? What would it look like to love that person “from God” this week?  
- What’s one specific way you can show love to someone in the next few days?  

## Verse + question for the week
**Verse:** "Beloved, let us love one another, for love is from God." — 1 John 4:7 (ESV)  
**Question:** Where does love come from?  

## Family take-home
**Do it together:** This week, each family member picks one way to show love to someone in your family, whether it’s helping with a chore, saying “I love you” at bedtime, or sharing something special. At the end of the week, reflect on how it went.
