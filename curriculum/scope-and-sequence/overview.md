# Scope & Sequence — Overview

A simple year plan so you can rotate through major Bible themes and books. Adjust dates and series length to fit your calendar.

---

## Series ideas (pick and order as you like)

| Series | Focus | Length | Good for |
|--------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Foundations** | God’s character, creation, fall, promise of a Savior | 4–6 weeks | Start of year; new believers |
| **Jesus: Life & Teachings** | Gospels — key stories, parables, and “I am” statements | 6–8 weeks | Any age |
| **Easter** | Holy Week, cross, resurrection | 2–4 weeks | Spring |
| **Acts & the Church** | Holy Spirit, early church, mission | 4–6 weeks | Kids+; great for youth/adults |
| **Fruit of the Spirit** | Galatians 5 — love, joy, peace, etc. | 9 weeks (one fruit per week) | All ages |
| **Psalms & Prayer** | Praying Scripture; lament and praise | 4–5 weeks | All ages |
| **Proverbs: Wisdom** | Fear of the Lord, choices, words, friendship | 4–6 weeks | Kids (simplified); youth/adults |
| **Advent / Christmas** | Prophecy, incarnation, hope | 4–5 weeks | December |

---

## Example year (52 weeks)

- **Jan:** Foundations (6 weeks)  
- **Feb–Mar:** Jesus: Life & Teachings (6 weeks)  
- **Apr:** Easter (3 weeks)  
- **May–Jun:** Acts & the Church (5 weeks)  
- **Jul–Aug:** Psalms & Prayer (4 weeks)  
- **Sep–Oct:** Fruit of the Spirit (9 weeks)  
- **Nov:** Proverbs: Wisdom (4 weeks)  
- **Dec:** Advent / Christmas (4 weeks)
